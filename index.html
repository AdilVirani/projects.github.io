Projects

I love to work on side projects in my free time. Here are some things I have worked on:
  • FreezeRay (created at Texas BitHack, April 2015) is a Wallet that stores money offline. We used multiple technologies to create this iOS app. Won:$1,150.
  • Hack.Connect (created at HackDFW and Mays App Challenge, March 2015) an iOS app that helps coders cooperate, convene, and code. Currently receiving MLH sponsorship and mentorship. Won: 3 years of workspace from Nöd and $2,000.
  • Code-Up (created at Hack@Brown, February 2015) another iOS app that helps teachers create sessions on the app, allowing students to join the session making it easier for the teacher to schedule appointments with students and maximize efficiency. Won: Pebble Smartwatch
  • CampusCalculator (created at Campus 2.0, November 2014) is an iOS Calculator app that connects with a website, which notifies a professor weather or not a student is on the app or not. Allowing the student to be able to use their calculator during an exam and the professor would know if he/she is cheating or not. Won: $400
  • EnergyPal (created at NTxAppsChallenge, October 2014) is a mobile app that displays real-time energy usage to homeowners. It displays the energy usage of various household appliances via wattmeters plugged into the outlets. Our goal is to make homeowners more conscious of their energy usage, thus reducing their energy consumption, thus saving money. Won: $10,000.
  • TripMaker (created at Sabre Hackathon, August 2014) a travel app that is designed to enable users to quickly and seamlessly plan for longer vacations and trips by helping them find cheap fares, explore locales, and to customize and construct their own itineraries all within a single application. Won: $7,500
